About Us

HAVANA is an annual Tech-Fest that brings together the brightest minds, hosted by the Department of Electrical, Electronics and Communication Engineering, GITAM. It is a National Level Inter-Institutional Technical Fest, which provides a platform to showcase the latest innovations and to share knowledge and skills.

HAVANA (in Sanskrit) means fire, which we imply as a strong desire, passion and exposure to knowledge. The two-day fest includes hands-on experience with projects, teaching competitive spirits, and developing & understanding the technologies; the event provides an experience for all. The fest is an excellent opportunity to network and make new connections. Attendees can discover new opportunities in the vibrant atmosphere and with contagious energy.

India's goal is indigenous technology DRDO scientist Rajesh N. Karwande Patancheru, Prabhanews: Indigenously designed, developed and Defense Research Organization (DRDO) scientist Rajesh S. Karwande said that our country is moving forward with the aim of achieving its own manufacturing technology. He was the chief guest at the two- day national inter-college technology festival Praram Bhotsava being held at Geetam Deemed University Hyderabad on Thursday under the name Havana-2.0. Speaking at the program organized under the auspices of the EECE department of the School of Technology, he advised the budding engineers to approach the DRDO if the students have any innovative ideas and inventions and need funds for them. DRDO Scientist felicitated Gitam students for their contribution in the development of 6 DOF model based MAT Lab. School of Business Director Professor Karunakar.B described Havana as a wonderful opportunity to learn new technologies. Resident Director DVVSR Verma urged that there is good everywhere and students should embrace it.
