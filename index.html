About Us

HAVANA is an annual Tech-Fest that brings together the brightest minds, hosted by the Department of Electrical, Electronics and Communication Engineering, GITAM. It is a National Level Inter-Institutional Technical Fest, which provides a platform to showcase the latest innovations and to share knowledge and skills.

HAVANA (in Sanskrit) means fire, which we imply as a strong desire, passion and exposure to knowledge. The two-day fest includes hands-on experience with projects, teaching competitive spirits, and developing & understanding the technologies; the event provides an experience for all. The fest is an excellent opportunity to network and make new connections. Attendees can discover new opportunities in the vibrant atmosphere and with contagious energy.
